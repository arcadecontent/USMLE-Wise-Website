Pricing & Payment

What each program costs and how payment works.

The Crash Course is $200 USD. This gives you 60 hours of high-quality recorded lectures for the USMLE step of your choice — Step 1, Step 2 CK, or Step 3.
Mastery with First Aid is $899 USD. This is a live, subject-by-subject coaching program built around the First Aid for USMLE Step 1 textbook.
NBME Classes (Live Coaching) are priced at $2,500 to $3,000 USD for the full syllabus. The exact price depends on your specific program package. Please reach out to our team for a personalized quote.
Payment plan availability varies by program. Please contact our team directly to discuss installment options. We want to make sure cost is never a barrier to your success.
Please contact our support team for details on our refund policy for each specific program. Our team will walk you through the terms applicable to your enrollment.
